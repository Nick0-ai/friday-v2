Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Friday

Getting Started

What is Friday?

Friday is an AI-powered file organization tool for Mac. It automatically scans, analyzes, and organizes your files into smart folders based on their content, type, and context. No more messy desktops!

How do I install Friday?

Simply download the DMG file from our homepage, open it, and drag Friday to your Applications folder. Launch it and you're ready to go!

Is Friday free?

Yes! Friday is completely free during our beta period. We'll introduce a Pro tier with advanced features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Privacy & Security

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Friday processes everything locally on your Mac. Your files never leave your device, and we never see, collect, or store any of your data. Privacy is our core principle.

Does Friday need internet access?

Friday works 100% offline. The AI runs locally on your Mac using on-device processing. No internet connection required for file organization.

What permissions does Friday need?

Friday only needs access to the folders you choose to organize. We request minimal permissions and never access files outside of your selected directories.

Features

Can I undo an organization?

Yes! Friday keeps a complete history of all file movements. You can undo any action with a single click, restoring files to their original locations instantly.

What file types does Friday support?

Friday supports all common file types including documents (PDF, Word, Pages), images (JPG, PNG, HEIC), videos, audio files, archives, and more. It intelligently categorizes each type.

Can I customize the organization rules?

Currently, Friday uses AI to determine the best organization. Custom rules are coming in our Pro tier, allowing you to create your own organization patterns.

Technical

What macOS versions are supported?

Friday requires macOS 12 (Monterey) or later. It's optimized for Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) but also runs on Intel Macs.

Why is Friday only for Mac?

We started with Mac to deliver the best possible experience on one platform first. Windows and Linux versions are on our roadmap for the future.
