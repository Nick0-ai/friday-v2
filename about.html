Not a dumb script. An intelligent personal assistant that thinks, orchestrates, and executes complete workflows from A to Z.

Concrete Example

"Friday, make me a presentation about Napoleon"

"It's for high school, 10 min presentation, text + slides."
Friday executes the complete workflow:
  • Opens ChatGPT or Claude, generates the perfect prompt
  • Waits for the response, analyzes quality
  • Opens Pages (or Word, or installs LibreOffice if needed)
  • Automatically formats: titles, subtitles, structure, readable font
  • Creates the PowerPoint from the text
  • Sends everything by email as PDF

"Friday, find me a dev internship in Strasbourg"

Friday asks the right questions, then:
  • Goes to LinkedIn, Indeed, Welcome to the Jungle
  • Creates an Excel with: Company, Position, Link, Salary
  • Offers to create your CV too
  • Sends everything organized to your inbox

"Friday, compare iPhone 15 Pro prices"

  • Goes to Amazon, Fnac, Darty, Apple
  • Compiles prices, sorts them
  • Highlights the best deal in green
  • "Best price: Fnac, $1099, free delivery."
