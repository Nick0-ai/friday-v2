Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 3, 2024

Privacy First

Friday V2 is designed with privacy at its core. All file analysis and organization happens 100% locally on your Mac. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your files or personal data.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At Friday V2, we believe your files are your business. Unlike cloud-based services, Friday V2 operates entirely on your local machine. This means:

  • Your files never leave your computer
  • No file contents are transmitted over the internet
  • No cloud processing of your personal data
  • No third-party access to your files

2. What We Don't Collect

Friday V2 does not collect:

  • File contents or names
  • Folder structures or paths
  • Personal documents, photos, or videos
  • Browsing history or application usage
  • Location data
  • Contact information from your files

3. What We May Collect

To improve our product and provide support, we may collect minimal, anonymous data:

3.1 Anonymous Usage Analytics

With your consent, we may collect:

  • App launch and crash reports
  • Feature usage statistics (e.g., number of files organized)
  • Performance metrics

This data is fully anonymized and cannot be linked to you or your files.

3.2 Account Information

If you create an account or purchase a license:

  • Email address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)
  • License key

4. Local Processing

All file analysis, classification, and organization is performed locally using:

  • On-device machine learning models
  • macOS native APIs (FileManager, Spotlight, Core ML)
  • Local database for preferences and history

No AI processing happens on external servers.

5. Data Storage

Friday V2 stores the following data locally on your Mac:

  • Application preferences and settings
  • Organization history (for undo functionality)
  • Custom rules and categories you create

This data is stored in your user Library folder and is never synced externally.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe - For secure payment processing
  • Vercel - For hosting our website

These services have their own privacy policies and do not have access to your files.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable all analytics collection in settings
  • Request deletion of your account data
  • Export your preferences and settings
  • Uninstall the app and remove all local data

8. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • macOS sandbox environment
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive settings
  • No network transmission of file data
  • Regular security audits

9. Children's Privacy

Friday V2 is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@usefriday.app
